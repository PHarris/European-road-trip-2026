Trip Summary

From Santander, head north-west along the Galician coast to La Coruña (1 night), then drive south to Porto/Matosinhos (2 nights) for city culture, great seafood and the Atlantic. Continue to Cascais (2 nights) for beaches and a Sintra day trip, then south to Lagos in the Algarve (4 nights — the longest stay) for sea caves and clifftop sunsets.

From Lagos head east to Seville (2 nights) for the Alcázar and flamenco, then north-east to Madrid (1 night) for the Prado and Puerta del Sol before the overnight drive or early morning run back to Santander for the 9 Aug ferry.

Estimated fuel: ~€400 · Tolls: ~€220 · Note: check Madrid Low Emission Zone compatibility for Defender TD5 (LJ51NOX) before arrival.
